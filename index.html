About This Project

This blog documents my individual project for EN8905. I have chosen SDG 13 – Climate Action as my focus SDG, supported by SDG 7 (Clean Energy) and SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities). The central question I investigate is:

"Should Bahrain and the wider MENA region transition from natural gas power generation to offshore wind energy — and what are the technical, economic, ethical, and social trade-offs?"

I am an electrical engineering student at Bahrain Polytechnic, and I work in the ICS department at SABIC. This professional context gives me direct insight into industrial energy consumption in the Gulf, making this investigation personally and professionally relevant.
